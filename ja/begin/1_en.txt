<h2><span>How to Start the Game</span></h2>

<p class="submenu"><a href="#b50154605b72a9385293277a65741880">System Requirements for Original Edition(OE)</a>　｜　<a href="#590bf696cc687eb4de2d1ebad096a9c8">System Requirements for High Grade Edition(HGE)</a>　｜　<a href="#f81fd2e4c52864042852c112ce927ae2">Download</a>　｜　<a href="#14b7d76f2abce310837a677aad65c1ad">Installation</a>　｜　<a href="#fda0a4c67b309c651de060fe1aedbad9">About the Launcher Screen</a>　｜　<a href="#29ae62f6121aa1cfa1b080fda4cd60f0">Log In and How to Start the Game</a>　｜　<a href="#255854d6cf8510c6c94f8efed221e618">How to Set up High Grade Edition</a></p>
<p class="leadtxt"> The PC version of "MHF-Z" has two editions as follows:<br>
ー The Original Edition(OE) which can be played in normal quality in a casual environment.<br>
ー The High Grade Edition(HGE) which allows users to experience powerful hunting in high quality.<br>
This guide will cover the system requirements and how to start the game, including download and installation.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p class="check">・You can choose to play MHF in either "OE" or "HGE" depending on your computer environments.<br />
・You don't need to have any courses and packages on playing MHF in "HGE".<br />
・Please note that the game may not start properly due to components, etc, even if your computer meets system requirements.</p>

<h3>Download</h3>
<div class="subsection">
<h4 class="head_dia">Procedure for Download</h4>
<p><strong>( 1 )</strong>First, if you already own a client (e.g. you have been playing on another server before), please delete the client file in advance to avoid unexpected problems. After deleting the file, please proceed to the next step.</p>
</div>
<div class="subsection">
<p><strong>( 2 )</strong>Download all-in-one client file from setup-navigation channel in Rain Official Discord.<br>(This includes all necessary files, so there is no need to download any patch files, etc.)</p>
</div>
<div class="subsection">
<p><strong>( 3 )</strong>Run "mhf.exe" in the client file downloaded in ( 2 ), and if you can see the launcher screen, the download is complete.</p>
</div>

<h3>Log In and How to Start the Game</h3>
<p class="leadtxt">After downloading the client, you can play MHF by logging in.</p>
<div class="subsection">
<h4 class="head_dia">Procedure for Login</h4>
<p><strong>( 1 )</strong>Click on "mhf.exe" in the client folder.</p>
<p class="notice check">If you are using a computer equipped with a multi-core processor, you can run multiple MHF at the same time.</p>
<a class="click" href="page37.html">Multiple Launch of the Game</a></div>

<div class="subsection">
<div class="imgBox"><img width="268" src="/sp/manual/image/18_20.jpg" alt="" />
<p><strong>( 2 )</strong>The launcher will be running.<br />
Enter your username and password in the red boxes in the above image and press the "Log In" to display your character information.</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="subsection">
<div class="imgBox">
<p class="clear-l">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="center"><img width="268" src="/sp/manual/image/18_21.jpg" alt="ランチャー画面" /></p>
<p><strong>( 3 )</strong>Press "Start Game" to log in to the game.</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="subsection">
<div class="imgBox"><img width="268" height="201" src="/sp/manual/image/18_22.jpg" alt="" />
<p><strong>( 4 )</strong>The game title screen will appear. Press Enter key or click on the screen to open the menu.</p>
</div>
</div>

<h3>How to Set up High Grade Edition</h3>
<p class="leadtxt">To enable HGE, the instructions are following:</p>
<div class="subsection">
<div class="imgBox"><img width="268" src="/sp/manual/image/18_23.jpg" alt="" />
<p><strong>( 1 )</strong>Click "Preferences" in the launcher.</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="subsection">
<div class="imgBox"><img width="268" src="/sp/manual/image/18_3.jpg" alt="" />
<p><strong>( 2 )</strong>You can see the settings screen. Check "Enable High Grade Edition" in the settings tab and click "OK".</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="subsection">
<div class="imgBox"><img width="268" src="/sp/manual/image/18_24.jpg" alt="" />
<p><strong>( 3 )</strong>After logging in, if "High Grade Edition" is displayed on the title screen, you can play the game in HGE.</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="subsection">
<h4 class="head_dia">In-game adjustments</h4>
<div class="imgBox"><img width="217" alt="" src="/sp/manual/image/18_10.jpg" />
<p>After moving to Mezeporta Square, graphics can be adjusted in [Menu]＞[Options]＞[Display (High  Grade)].</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="subsection">
<div class="imgBox"><img width="500" alt="" src="/sp/manual/image/18_7.jpg" /></div>
<h4 class="head_dia">More about "Display (High  Grade)"</h4>
<h4>１．Presets</h4>
<p>"Maximum": Set to the highest settings that all various items turned on.</p>
<p>"Quality Priority": Set to a high-definition setting that prioritizes graphics.</p>
<p>"Performance Priority": Set a low load setting that prioritizes ease of operation.</p>
<p>"Custom": Various items can be set freely.</p>
<br />
<h4>２．Various Items</h4>
<p>"Realistic Shadows (Town)": Shadow setting for hunters and NPCs in the town.</p>
<p>"Realistic Shadows (Quest)": Shadow setting for hunters, monsters, etc. in quests.</p>
<p>"Depth of Field": Blurring the background when looking at objects up close can enhance the immersive experience.</p>
<p>"High Dynamic Range": More realistic expression of light effect.</p>
<p>"SSAO": Reflects more natural shading of shadows.</p>
<p>"God Rays": Expresses light coming through the crack or the leaves.</p>
<p>"Anti-Aliasing": Smoothly describe the outline of objects.</p>
<p>"Soft Particles": Smoothly describe the outline of objects.</p>
