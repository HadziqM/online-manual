<p>Start the game by freely selecting worlds.</p>

<p>Can configure key settings and other items relevant to gameplay.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><a name="01-1"></a></p>
<h4 class="head_arrow">Settings in the Options</h4>


<div class="subsection">
<div class="imgBox"><img src="/images/manual/static/30/02.jpg" height="255" alt="" width="340">
<p>In the options, you can configure key setting for keyboard or gamepad, sound, screen brightness, etc.</p></div>
</div>


<div class="subsection">
<h4> [Game Settings] </h4>
<div class="imgBox"><img src="/images/manual/static/30/03.jpg" border="0" height="255" alt="" width="340">
<p>You can configure settings related to displays, such as camera and scope during the game.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="subsection">
<h4>Vibration</h4>
<p>Toggles the gamepad vibration function on and off when using a gamepad (controller).</p>
<h4>Scope Controls</h4>
<p>Change the control of the scope when using a bowgun.</p>
<h4>Camera Controls</h4>
<p>Change the control of the camera during the quest.</p>
<h4>Bowgun Camera Type</h4>
<p>Change the camera type for gunner-type hunters equipped with a bowgun or bow when aiming.</p>
<h4>Quick Aim Controls</h4>
<p>Change the simple aiming controls type for gunner-type hunters equipped with a bowgun or bow.</p>
</div>

<div class="subsection">
<p><a name="01-2"></a></p>
<h4>[Key Settings]</h4>
<div class="imgBox"><img src="/images/manual/static/30/04.jpg" border="0" height="255" alt="" width="340">
<p>Can change keyboard, gamepad, and mouse control assignments. The "Normal Action" and "System" can be changed and initialized for each of the 10 weapon types. Also, can adjust the analog sticks on the gamepad.</p>

</div>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h4> [Screen Brightness] </h4>
<p>Adjust the brightness of the screen.</p>
